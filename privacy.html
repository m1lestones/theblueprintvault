Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

1. Introduction

The Blueprint Vault ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, payment information
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your purchases
  • Send you product downloads and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

4. Payment Processing

All payment information is processed securely through Stripe. We do not store credit card information on our servers. Stripe handles all payment data in accordance with PCI DSS standards.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Netlify: Website hosting

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

  • Data portability
